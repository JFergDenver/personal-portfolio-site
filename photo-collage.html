notes

Photos are loaded locally in your browser and never uploaded anywhere. Switch layouts with the dropdown above — extra tiles are hidden automatically. "Download as image" renders the collage to a PNG using the canvas API.

To preset photos for each tile, drop image files into images/collage/ and reference them by filename in js/collage-config.js — e.g. 1: "beach.jpg". Configured tiles load automatically when the page opens. Clicking a filled tile opens a larger preview; drag a new image onto a tile to replace its photo.
